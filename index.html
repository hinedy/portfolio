About Me

Hey there, I'm Ahmed Hinedy, a frontend developer with a unique perspective. My journey into the world of development has been anything but straightforward. After earning my bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering and honing my creative skills through years of graphic design work, I discovered my true passion lay at the intersection of aesthetics and functionality - in the world of frontend development.

Get to know me!

As someone who appreciates beauty and aesthetics, I approach my work with a keen eye for design and a love of clean, efficient code. But what sets me apart is my commitment to slow living - the idea that life is best enjoyed when we take the time to savor each moment. This philosophy has shaped my approach to everything, from the way I work to the way I live.

Whether I'm working on a project, spending time with my plants, or cooking up a storm in the kitchen, I approach each moment with intention and purpose. I believe that taking the time to appreciate the details is what sets the best work apart - and that's what I strive to bring to every project I work on.

So if you're looking for a frontend developer who can bring a unique perspective to your team - someone with a background in engineering and design, a passion for beauty and efficiency - then I'm your guy. Let's create something beautiful together!
